Scores & Rules

Game Rules:

1. The board has 22 columns and 38 rows. The left margin (column 0) is a closed wall, while the top, bottom, and right borders are open.

2. Two players, Mino (Blue) and Chen (Red), take turns placing points on intersections.

3. A group of enemy points is considered encircled if they are completely enclosed by active points of the opponent.

4. Only enemy groups that contain at least one enemy point count for scoring.

5. When an encirclement is detected, the border is drawn by connecting the opponent’s active points along the border.

6. Encircled points become disabled: although they remain visible, they cannot be used to form new encirclements.

7. Clicking is only accepted if the mouse is close enough to an intersection center.
